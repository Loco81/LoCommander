<p align="center"> LoCommander, your offline voice commander!🤝 </p>
<p align="center"> An offline Persian or English voice assistant for your Windows. It runs with your system startup and performs any command you give it in the background!😎 From telling the date and time and shutting down or standby the system to playing and pausing or playing the next music or movie, searching for what you say to it, telling the hardware temperature, voice typing, testing the Internet connection, adjusting the volume and brightness, opening a specific program or folder, and ... If you need a voice assistant for your Windows, don't miss this program☺️<br>⭕Note⭕<br>How to use: After installing and running the program, wake the program by saying \"Hey Loco\" or typing Loco on the keyboard or the Ctrl+Alt+L key combination and say your command or type in the activated window (English). To change your speech language to Persian or other settings, click on the program icon in the hidden icons section on the right side of the taskbar (system tray). To see a list of commands you can give to the program, visit the program settings section. </p>
